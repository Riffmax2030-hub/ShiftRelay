# ShiftRelay Monetization

ShiftRelay should sell to organisations, not individual workers. Workers receive access through their employer.

## Plans

| Plan | Target customer | Suggested price | Includes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Starter | Small teams | Free for up to 10 active staff | Handovers, basic shifts, 30-day history |
| Team | Growing operations | US$4 per active staff/month | Approval workflows, attendance, calendar, exports, multilingual interface |
| Operations | Multi-site organisations | US$7 per active staff/month | Custom workflows, analytics, incident reporting, role controls, priority support |
| Enterprise | Regulated/global teams | Quote-based | SSO, audit retention, API, dedicated onboarding, custom data retention |

## Billing rules

- Charge the organisation owner monthly by active staff count.
- Give a 14-day Team trial; do not require a card for the demo.
- Use Stripe Billing or a regional payment provider only after a legal business entity, tax setup, refund policy, and production support email are ready.
- Do not use Apple or Google in-app purchases for employer SaaS plans sold and managed outside the mobile apps; confirm current store rules with legal/payment advice before enabling mobile purchase flows.

## First sales motion

1. Start with logistics, retail, healthcare support, hospitality, or security teams with recurring shift handovers.
2. Offer one department a 30-day pilot with success metrics: completion rate, late handovers, incident follow-up, and time saved.
3. Convert the organisation to Team after demonstrating fewer missed tasks and clearer accountability.

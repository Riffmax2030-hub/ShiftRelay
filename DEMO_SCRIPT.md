# ShiftRelay 3-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00–0:20 — Problem

"Shift change is where important work can get lost. Paper notes, rushed calls, and unclear ownership leave the next worker without the full picture. ShiftRelay makes each handover clear, reviewed, and accountable."

## 0:20–0:45 — Organisation setup

Show the landing page and organisation registration. Explain that organisations receive a unique ID, then workers request access with their work details. The owner or workforce manager approves and assigns roles.

## 0:45–1:25 — Outgoing worker

Sign in as the outgoing worker. Show the dashboard, clock-in status, and click **New handover**. Enter or use the prepared update, then generate the handover. Say: "GPT-5.6 Sol structures unstructured shift notes into risks, owners, deadlines, and missing context. The supervisor still decides what moves forward."

## 1:25–1:55 — Supervisor

Switch to the supervisor. Open the review queue, show the checklist, confirm the key facts, and relay it. Explain that ShiftRelay records the action and notifies the next person.

## 1:55–2:20 — Incoming worker

Switch to the incoming worker. Open the incoming checklist, show priority actions, and acknowledge the handover. Mention the app preserves a traceable history instead of relying on paper notes.

## 2:20–2:45 — Owner view

Switch to the owner dashboard. Show staff approvals, analytics, calendar, attendance, and the account menu. Mention multilingual preferences and the global time-zone foundation.

## 2:45–3:00 — Close

"I used Codex to rapidly build the full product flow: database-backed roles, responsive interface, handover workflow, notifications, voice-transcription integration, and launch documentation. ShiftRelay gives frontline organisations a clearer, safer way to move work forward."

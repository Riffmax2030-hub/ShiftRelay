# ShiftRelay Store Listing Kit

## App name

ShiftRelay — Shift Handover

## Short description

Clear, accountable shift handovers for frontline teams.

## Full description

ShiftRelay helps frontline teams hand work over without losing critical context. Workers capture a handover by writing or recording an update; ShiftRelay turns it into a structured brief with priorities, owners, deadlines, and missing context. Supervisors review before the incoming worker receives and acknowledges the work.

Organisations can approve staff access, assign roles and workflows, schedule shifts, track clock-in/out time, view workforce activity, report incidents, and maintain a clear handover history. The app supports global teams with country/time-zone setup and multilingual interface preferences.

ShiftRelay is built for organisations in logistics, retail, hospitality, healthcare support, security, manufacturing, and other shift-based operations.
